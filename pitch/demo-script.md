# 3-minute Demo Script

## 0:00-0:20 Opening

Community Spirit is a community life RPG engine grounded in real spatial operations.

Tonight, an underused basketball court needs two more players. Instead of posting another message in a noisy group chat, Community Pulse finds residents who like basketball, are free tonight, and prefer activity-first social connection.

Use the Hackathon Demo controller at the top. Click **Reset story**, then advance with **Run next step**.

## 0:20-0:55 Community Pulse Match

Show the live Community Pulse card:

- why the event matches this resident;
- tonight's time and four-minute walking distance;
- the current participant gap;
- the low-pressure privacy rule;
- the shared goal to unlock a community movie night.

Click **Join the pulse**.

The demo controller automatically moves from Resident World to Task & Benefits.

## 0:55-1:25 Real-space Execution

Show that joining selects a real task, reserves a participant slot, and highlights the route from Building 3 to the basketball court.

Point out that the same structured objects connect resident intent to a POI, route, activity, schedule, safety rules, and expected outputs.

Click **Simulate court check-in**.

## 1:25-1:50 Identity and Shared World

Show the result:

- resident XP increases;
- Sports Neighbor badge progress advances;
- the community movie-night goal receives both join and check-in points;
- the event changes from a recommendation into a completed real-world action.

The reward is not only a digital badge. Residents collectively unlock something that can happen in the physical community.

Continue the guided steps to claim, activate, and redeem the contextual sports benefit with Spirit Points. Show the synthetic resident benefit pass, its claim-to-redemption timeline, and the itemized wallet entries that preserve the resulting balance.

## 1:50-2:20 World Ops

Scroll to World Ops and show the same event as an operating funnel:

The final guided step switches directly to the World Ops view.

- recommendations;
- detail opens;
- confirmed joins;
- actual check-ins;
- first participation, 7-day return, and current streaks;
- projected basketball-court activation.

Briefly show the Pulse lifecycle control: full and expired stop new joins without removing confirmed participants, while ended closes check-in. Restore the Pulse to matching before resetting the story.

Explain that property teams are not buying another dashboard. They are buying a repeatable way to turn underused spaces into measurable participation and repeat visits.

## 2:20-2:45 Living World Depth

Briefly show:

- Community Explorer onboarding;
- scheduled parent-child and world-maintenance side quests;
- resident identity and Season Arc;
- privacy-aware issue reporting.

These are additional stories running on the same spatial quest engine.

## 2:45-3:00 Reality Layer

Open the robot patrol workflow and show the structured JSON export.

Close with:

> Community Spirit turns real community spaces into events, relationships, shared goals, and measurable operations.

All current data is fictional or synthetic. A real pilot requires permissioned resident data, verified spaces, consent, and property approval.
