# Code of Conduct

Community Spirit and CACP are early public prototypes. The project welcomes constructive review, but discussions must stay respectful, specific, and safety-aware.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Critique ideas, code, docs, examples, and safety boundaries without attacking people.
- Keep feedback concrete: point to the file, behavior, schema, example, or validator output.
- Respect the synthetic-data boundary. Do not ask contributors to publish real resident, property, access-control, camera, sensor, payment, or robot-operation data.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private or sensitive real-world community data.
- Encouraging surveillance, unsafe robot deployment, or bypassing consent / operator approval.
- Misrepresenting the public demo as a production system or real pilot.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block contributors who violate these expectations.

For security or privacy concerns, follow `SECURITY.md`.
